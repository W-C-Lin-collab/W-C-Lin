Research & Projects

Undergraduate Research / Fiber Sensing Lab

Optical Fiber Sensing and Magnetic-Fluid Devices · 2023 – Present

  • Conducted research on magnetic-fluid-filled fiber Fabry–Pérot and anti-resonant structures for magnetic field sensing.
  • Responsibilities include magnetic fluid synthesis, Helmholtz-coil system setup, optical spectrum measurements, and data analysis for sensitivity and extinction-ratio evaluation.

Hollow-Core Fiber Mach–Zehnder Interferometers

High-Extinction-Ratio FMZI Design · 2024 –

  • Participated in the design and characterization of FMZIs based on connections of discrepant hollow-core fibers.
  • Focused on ultrahigh extinction ratio and ultrabroadband single-dip spectra for temperature sensing and refractive-index measurement.
