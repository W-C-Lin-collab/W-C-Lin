Experience

Research & Projects

  • Undergraduate Research / Fiber Sensing Lab

    Optical Fiber Sensing and Magnetic Fluid Devices

    Conducted research on magnetic-fluid-filled fiber Fabry–Pérot and anti-resonant structures for magnetic field sensing. Responsibilities include magnetic fluid synthesis, Helmholtz-coil system setup, optical spectrum measurements, and data analysis for sensitivity and extinction ratio evaluation.

  • Hollow-Core Fiber Mach–Zehnder Interferometers

    Participated in the design and characterization of FMZIs based on connections of discrepant hollow-core fibers, focusing on ultrahigh extinction ratio and ultrabroadband single-dip spectra for temperature sensing.
